## ğŸ® How to Play
Controls
Mouse Left Click: Interact with cards in your hand, the draw deck, or the color picker.

Hover: Hover over cards in your hand to lift them for better visibility.

'R' Key: Restart the game once a player has won.

## Game Rules
Matching: Play a card that matches the top card's color or number/type.

Wilds: Can be played at any time. You will be prompted to select a new color.

Drawing: If you cannot play, click the Face-down Deck to draw a card and pass your turn.

Winning: The first player to empty their hand wins the game!
